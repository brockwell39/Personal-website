Upon graduation Alex decided to start his own business. He started a location catering company called Oranges Location Catering. Which provides catering for film crews.

He started by helping out his friend on his graduate short film down in bournemouth. Where he cooked breakfast and lunch for 30 students for a week. He quite enjoyed this so decided to see how far he could take it.

Alex started by renting a small catering trailer and pitching to music video producers, because this is very price sensitive market he was able to undercut the competition.

Slowly he managed to build up a list of contacts and turnover grew, he bought his own catering trailer on a lease deal and took on a member of staff.

After a few years he decided to take on a chef who would take on the cooking responsibilities and Alex could concentrate on the running of the business.

He invested in new equipment and took out a lease on a light industrial unit in Brixton. Turnover again grew. Below is a table of the turnover over the years and shows how he has managed to grow the turnover.
